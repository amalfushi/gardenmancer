# 1.4 Plant Seed Data — Plan

Create a seed data file (`src/lib/data/seed-plants.ts`) containing approximately 50 common garden plants with complete metadata: name, variety, scientific name, USDA zones, spacing (inches), height range, sun requirements, water needs, days to germination, days to harvest, planting depth, companion plants, antagonist plants, and season-specific planting instructions. Focus on popular vegetables, herbs, and flowers.
